Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, please go to the login page and click the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

Where can I find the user manual?

The latest user manual is available for download in the 'Documents' section of your account portal, or you can view it online here (insert link).

What are the system requirements?

Clearly requires Windows 10/11 or macOS Monterey (12.0) or later, at least 8GB of RAM, and 500MB of free disk space. An internet connection is required for activation and updates.

How do I update to the latest version?

Typically, Clearly will prompt you automatically when a new update is available. You can also manually check for updates by going to the 'Help' menu and selecting 'Check for Updates'.
